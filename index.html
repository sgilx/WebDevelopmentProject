Our Purpose

We aim to bring you the most relevant and useful information regarding nursing in order to make your journey down this path easier. By gathering information about institutions around the country, we are able to bring it to you in an organized manner. From the different educational levels necessary, to programs to consider and other paths you could take, we are committed to helping ease the weight of these decisions.

To explore more, visit the American Nursing Association website.

Things to Consider

Oustide of hard skills, nurses are expected to have:

  • Social & Communication skills
  • Interpersonal Skills
  • Open to learning
  • Humility & Confidence

Things Employers will look for include:

  • Asking for help when you need it
  • Doing your own research; teaching yourself
  • Asking questions in general

Don't be overly confident, you do not know more than the veterans.
